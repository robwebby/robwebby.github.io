ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This is my website of stuff I've done in R relating to UK Elections. It's more a platform for me to test my skills in HTML, R, etc than anything serious. Any questions feel free to tweet me, which you can do by clicking the twitter tab.

For the upcoming election on the 8th June, I will be updating maps and figures through the night, so check back then to see if I can keep up!!

ABOUT THE MAPS

Below are standard maps for General Elections back to 2010 and swing graphics for 2015 and 2017. Also included is a cartogram which treats all constituencies as equal physical area, which makes visual interpretation of who as the most seats easier. The results coming in won't be as quick as from a media outlet, but being a bit of a political geek I'm staying up anyway and this will keep me busy!

Other Brilliant Sites

Election Forecast - This election forcast by Chris Hanretty at UEA is fab for checking out how they look to be going based upon his model that included British Election Study data alongside the polls in the run up to the actual election.

When Will Seats Declare? - In terms of when to expect results, this cool tool by Global Counsel is brilliant!
